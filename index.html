SeraAI is a personal skincare wellness application that provides:

  • AI-Powered Skin Analysis: Using your device's camera, SeraAI analyzes facial images to provide cosmetic appearance-based skin assessments including scores for metrics such as acne, texture, radiance, pores, dark spots, redness, wrinkles, and under-eye appearance.
  • Personalized Skincare Routine Tracking: Tools to build, manage, and track your daily and weekly skincare routines, including step scheduling, completion tracking, and streak monitoring.
